About us

Welcome to Houseplant Assistant, your go-to web app for personalized houseplant care advice! Designed for plant enthusiasts of all levels, our platform leverages the Perennia API to provide expert tips and reliable information on a variety of houseplant species. Whether you need guidance on watering schedules, light requirements, or general plant care, we're here to help your indoor garden thrive.
